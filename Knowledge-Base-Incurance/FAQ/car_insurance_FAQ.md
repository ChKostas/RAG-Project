# Car Insurance Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. What is this product about?
This policy provides insurance against damage to your own vehicle and/or your liabilities to other parties as a result of a motor accident, depending on the type of cover you selected.

## 2. What are the covers / benefits provided?
### a) Comprehensive Cover
- Accidental or fire damage to your vehicle
- Theft of your vehicle

### b) Third Party Fire & Theft
- Cover your liability towards third parties as a result of a motor accident
- Damage or loss of your vehicle due to fire and/or theft

### c) Third Party Cover
- Covers:
  - Death or third-party bodily injury
  - Third-party property loss or damage

### Optional add-on benefits available with Comprehensive Cover (pay additional premium):
- Windscreen damage
- Riot, Strike & Civil Commotion
- Passenger liability cover
- Damage arising from flood and landslide
- Cash compensation if your vehicle is declared a total loss or damaged due to flood
- Transportation fare
- Vehicle break-in
- Key replacement
- Car loan support
- Tyres and rims repair/replacement

*Note:* The above optional benefits are for Private Cars only. Separate extras are available for Commercial Vehicles.

- Duration of cover is one year, requiring annual renewal. It is advisable that the insurance period matches the road tax validity. To check your car insurance expiry date, visit JPJ.

- We also provide free 24-hour, 7-day Road Assistance Service for all Comprehensive Car Insurance policies.

## 3. How do I renew my auto insurance?
Renew online, including road tax renewal service, via the B Infinite website.

## 4. How does the road tax renewal service work when renewing online?
We provide **Road Tax renewal service with FREE Delivery** when you renew your auto insurance online with us.

### Is the road tax renewal service applicable for vehicles used in West Malaysia & East Malaysia?
- This service is currently available only for vehicles used in West Malaysia.
- For vehicles used in East Malaysia, you can still renew your auto insurance online (without selecting road tax).

### What charges are involved when renewing road tax online?
- You will only be charged for the road tax fee (West Malaysia rate applies).
- No charges for delivery or service fees, as this is a complimentary service by AIG.

### When will I receive my road tax after renewal?
- During Hari Raya Holidays, no road tax deliveries from 21/05/20 to 26/05/20.
- Delivery times:
  - Klang Valley: 6 working days
  - Peninsular/West Malaysia: 7 working days
  - Sabah & Sarawak: within 9 working days
- If delivery is delayed, you may use the road tax receipt as proof of renewal.

### How to obtain the road tax receipt?
Visit [https://rdtax.myeg.com.my/rtxchk/rtxform.jsp](https://rdtax.myeg.com.my/rtxchk/rtxform.jsp).

### Who will deliver the road tax?
All deliveries are facilitated by MyEG Services Logistic Officers.

### What if I haven't received my road tax as expected?
- Check your delivery status via:
  - Online at MyEG website
  - Call MyEG customer service at +603.7801 8888
  - Call AIG Malaysia at 1800 88 8811

## 5. How much premium do I have to pay?
Premiums are based on risk factors such as age, gender, vehicle age, make and model, cubic capacity, sum insured, location of use, and other applicable factors at the time of cover issuance.

## 6. What are the fees and charges?
- Stamp duty: RM10
- Premiums include a 6% Service Tax (ST)

## 7. What are some key terms and conditions?
- **Eligibility:** Anyone aged 22 to 75 with a valid driving license for the appropriate vehicle class, minimum 1 year driving experience. Acceptance is subject to underwriting guidelines.
- **Disclosure:** Under Paragraph 5 of Schedule 9 of the Financial Services Act 2013, you must answer all questions fully and accurately, and disclose all relevant information. Failure to do so may result in policy avoidance, claim refusal, or termination.
- **Duty of Disclosure:** Continues until the policy is entered into, varied, or renewed.
- **Additional Disclosures:** You must inform us immediately if any information provided changes or is found to be inaccurate.
- **Excess:** RM400 additional excess applies if the vehicle is driven by a person not named in the policy, under 21, or on a probationary license (for Comprehensive Private Car).
- **Betterment Charges:** Apply for vehicles aged 5 years and above when parts are replaced with new parts.
- **Insurance Amount:** Ensure your vehicle is insured at an appropriate value.
- **Vehicle Condition:** Maintain your vehicle in a roadworthy condition.
- **Payment:** Motor insurance is a Cash Before Cover policy; payment must be made upfront.

## 8. What are the major exclusions?
This policy does not cover:
- Your own death or bodily injury due to a motor accident
- Liability against claims from passengers
- Loss, damage, or liability arising from acts of nature such as floods, storms, or landslides

*Note:* This list is non-exhaustive. Refer to the policy contract for full exclusions.

## 9. Can I cancel my policy?
Yes, you may cancel at any time by written notice. Refunds are available if no claim has been paid. A minimum premium of RM50 will be retained, and the remaining premium will be prorated based on the policy period.
